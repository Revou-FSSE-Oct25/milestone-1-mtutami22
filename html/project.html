Top 50 Best Selling Novels 2009-2021 of Amazon

  • Analyzed Amazon's 650 bestselling books dataset (2009-2021) using Python to identify key drivers of book popularity and quality through genre, price, rating, and review analysis.
  • Discovered Non-Fiction books dominate the market and found a significant negative correlation between book prices and user ratings, providing crucial insights for pricing strategy.
  • Conducted inferential statistical testing (Chi-Square Test) confirming that genre significantly influences ratings, enabling focus on high-performing genres.
  • Visualized a substantial increase in reviews during 2020-2021, indicating growing market opportunities through online platforms.

Tools: pandas, scipy, matplotlib, seaborn, numpy, tableau.

Boost Local Skills to Compete in Global Market: Freelancer Profile Analysis

  • Conducted a comprehensive analysis of a 1,000-freelancer global dataset to assess Indonesia's competitive position and develop a targeted training strategy to break into the top 10 rankings.
  • Identified critical skill gaps, finding that Indonesian freelancers lag in DevOps and Machine Learning, despite having competitive UI/UX Design skills and high hourly rates.
  • Discovered a key service quality issue, where high rates are not matched by client satisfaction, indicating a need for improved service delivery beyond technical skills.
  • Developed data-driven strategic recommendations, including a DevOps bootcamp and Machine Learning certification, supported by an interactive Tableau dashboard for strategic decision-making.

Tools: pandas, scipy, matplotlib, seaborn, numpy, Tableau.
